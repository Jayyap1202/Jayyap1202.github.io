Introduction

Terengganu formerly spelled Trengganu or Tringganu, is a sultanate and constitutive state of federal Malaysia. The state is also known by its Arabic honorific, Dāru l-Īmān ("Abode of Faith"). There are many islands located close to the coast of Terengganu state, such as Perhentian Islands and Redang. Island.

Terengganu is located in the southern half of the Malay Peninsula, across the South China Sea from East Malaysia (on the island of Borneo).
